# 🎬 FlexCode - 3-Minute Pitch Script

> **Total Time: 3-4 minutes**
> - Introduction: 30 seconds
> - Demo: 2 minutes
> - Vision: 30 seconds

---

## 🎤 PART 1: Introduction (30 seconds)

### Opening Hook
> *"Every business runs on workflows. But here's the problem — Zapier connects apps, not intelligence. What if your automations could think, compete, and actually learn from mistakes?"*

### The Problem
> *"Today, when you automate customer support or sales follow-ups, you're just connecting boxes. One template. One response. No learning. If it fails, you fix it manually."*

### Our Solution
> *"Meet **FlexCode** — the first no-code platform where AI agents collaborate as a team. They compete to give you the best response, a supervisor scores them, and the system learns from your feedback. It's Zapier, but with a brain."*

### 📧 DEMO 1: Customer Support Triage (60 seconds)

#### Setup (10 sec)
> *"Let me show you. Here's our dashboard — clean, minimal, no clutter. I've got a workflow called 'Support Triage' already set up."*

**[Click on Workflows tab, show the dashboard with stats]**

#### Trigger Input (10 sec)
> *"A frustrated customer emails us — they were charged twice. Let me run this through our pipeline."*

**[Click Run → Paste demo email:]**
```
Subject: URGENT - Charged twice for my subscription!
From: angry.customer@gmail.com

I just noticed TWO charges of $49.99 on my credit card from your service. 
I only have ONE account! This is ridiculous. I've been a customer for 2 years 
and this is how you treat me? Fix this NOW or I'm filing a dispute.
```

**[Click Execute Pipeline]**

#### Pipeline Running (15 sec)
> *"Watch the pipeline — first, our Classifier categorizes this as 'Billing Issue, High Priority'. Now three competing agents draft responses in parallel — one formal, one empathetic, one urgent."*

**[Show pipeline progress animation with stages]**

#### Results (15 sec)
> *"Here are the results. The Arena shows all three drafts with scores. The empathetic agent won — look, score 87 vs 72 and 68. The decision agent picked it because our RL weights favor this style for billing complaints."*

**[Show Arena cards with winner badge]**

#### Feedback (10 sec)
> *"I click Accept — and that's it. The system just learned. Next billing complaint? It'll prefer the empathetic style even more. No retraining. No code. Just human feedback."*

**[Click thumbs up, show feedback toast]**

---

### 💼 DEMO 2: Sales Lead Qualification (60 seconds)

#### Setup (10 sec)
> *"Now let's look at sales. Same platform, different workflow. This one qualifies inbound leads and routes them."*

**[Navigate to Workflows, click on 'Lead Qualifier' workflow]**

#### Trigger Input (10 sec)
> *"Here's a lead that just came in through our form."*

**[Click Run → Paste:]**
```
Company: TechStartup Inc.
Contact: Sarah Chen, VP Engineering
Employees: 150
Message: "We're scaling our support team and need to automate ticket routing. 
Currently using Zendesk but drowning in volume. Budget approved for Q1."
```

**[Click Execute]**

#### Results (20 sec)
> *"The Analyzer extracts key signals — 150 employees, VP-level contact, approved budget, pain point identified. Three agents score this lead differently:"*
> - *"Agent 1: 'Hot Lead - Demo Ready' — score 92"*
> - *"Agent 2: 'Warm Lead - Needs Nurture' — score 75"*  
> - *"Agent 3: 'Qualified but Long Cycle' — score 70"*

> *"The winner? Hot Lead. It's routing to our sales channel right now."*

**[Show the Arena with lead qualification scores]**

#### Learning Loop (20 sec)
> *"Here's the magic — see these RL weights? Agent 1 started at 33%. After 50 leads and feedback, it's now at 47%. The system learned that for tech companies with approved budgets, Agent 1's aggressive qualification works better."*

**[Show RL Insights tab with weight bars]**

> *"That's adaptive AI. Not templates. Not rules. Learning."*
